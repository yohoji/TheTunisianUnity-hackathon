For Feres, growing up in poverty meant living with heavy burdens that no child should have to carry.

As Feres's parents struggled to feed their family, Feres did everything he could to help.

He got a job bring in extra money, but that left him no time for his school work, so he began to fall behind.

By making a gift or becoming a child sponsor today, you can help lift the burden for children like Feres, and give them the chance they deserve to fulfill their potential.

A Boy like him deserves your support. A donation from you can ensure he continues chasing his dreams with full vigour.

This is Ahmed, 10 years old, one of 6 siblings.

Ahmed lives in a large marketplace in Tunisia with his mother and his two younger sisters. They sleep under a piece of plastic on a blanket amongst other families.

Ahmed has spent most of his life on the streets begging for food and money for him and his family, as a result, he didn't have time to go to school.

By donating for him or becoming a child sponsor, childs like Ahmed will be able to start going back to school and ensure their future.
